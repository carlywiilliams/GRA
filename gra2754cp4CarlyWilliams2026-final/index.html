About Me

I’m a 23-year-old graphic designer currently completing my Associate’s Degree in Graphic Design, which I will be graduating with this May. Over the past two years, I’ve developed a strong foundation in design and visual storytelling while gaining experience working with Adobe programs including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Lightroom, and After Effects. Alongside my studies, I’m starting a small creative business where I combine crafting, design, and photography, while also exploring photo editing and visual content creation. This upcoming summer, I will be interning at my mom’s law firm, working closely with their marketing team to help develop and manage social media content. My long-term dream is to work for a leading publication such as Vogue or Architectural Digest, where I can combine my passion for design, branding, and visual storytelling in a creative and editorial environment.
